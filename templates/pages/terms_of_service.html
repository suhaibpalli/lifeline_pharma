5. Prescription Requirements

Prescription Medicines

For medicines marked as prescription-required (Rx), you must upload a valid prescription from a registered medical practitioner. Our pharmacists will verify the prescription before dispensing.

Valid prescriptions must include:

  • Doctor's name and registration number
  • Date of prescription
  • Medicine name, dosage, and duration
  • Doctor's signature and stamp
